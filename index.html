We save koalas by protecting their natural habitat, pushing koala protection bills and conserving existing koala populations

Koala Habitat

 80% of the Australian eucalypt forests have been cleared and almost none of the 20% remaining are being protected. Learn more about land clearing and koala habitat destruction here..

Koala Protection Act

 There is currently no legisation that protects our national icon, practically encouraging land clearing and the destruction of koala habitat. New koala friendly legislation must be drafted to actively protect these animals.

Conservation and Research

 We the AKF have conducted many research projects that provide the infomation neccesary for the survival of the koala species. From habitat mapping, population estimates and the Act or Axe initative.
